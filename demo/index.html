Cleanup & Restoration

ABC Emergency Response is a 24/7 Disaster Team providing fire, water, smoke and other mitigation, cleaning, and reconstruction services. We service the Capital District and Greater Upstate New York. From call to completion we work directly with your insurance company and provide quality services ranging from drying and dehumidification to cleaning and construction restoration.

Emergency Disaster & Construction Services from A-Z

Asbestos Abatement:

We provide quality NYS Licensed, Bonded, & Insured Asbestos Abatements. Our crews are conscientious professionals who work diligently to restore your home, business, or municipality.

Basement Flooding:

Time is of the Essence in a Water related loss. Mold can begin to grow on in as little as (24-48 hours). Our Emergency Response and dispatch of crews can prevent further property damage during a flood. Our staff is professionally cross-trained in drying techniques (IICRC), mold, asbestos and selective demolition. Our water extraction, drying equipment, and methods are state-of-the-art. We employ all necessary methods to ensure loss-prevention and maintain customer satisfaction.

Building Collapse:

If your business or home has collapsed as a result of a heavy snow-load or due to an automobile collision, it is a daunting task to deal with the insurance company claims process. We work with all major insurance carriers to produce proper scopes of work and rebuild of the structure, to code, to ensure longevity of repairs. We accept 3rd party payments directly from insurer handle all aspects of the project.

Construction Services:

ABC provides design-build construction services for industrial, commercial, and residential property owners. From contents and furniture pack-out to the final cleaning we provide all services needed to get you back into your home or business.

Demolition:

We are capable of total loss demolition services for condemned structures as well as selective demolition services as needed for reconstruction of restoration projects. Full-scale in-house demolition and hauling with Hazmat endorsements.

Emergency Board-up:

In the event of a fire loss, fallen tree, or wind damage you may need temporary services including a board-up to keep people and critters from entering the home or business until permanent repairs are made. We are available 24/7 to help mitigate property loss and enclose properties where they are otherwise breached.

Fire Damage:

Our goal at ABC Emergency Response is to get you back into your home or business as quickly as possible. A fire of any size is an unexpected loss that can leave your home and your life in disarray. Our goal is to minimize the impact this loss will have. Your safety is a priority. Prior to calling ABC Emergency Response, please contact your local fire department or 911. After a fire is properly extinguished, our crews can be on stand-by 24/7 to assess the damage, create a Mission Critical work-plan, coordinate with Engineering, and provide moving (pack-out) services for your possessions, furniture and miscellaneous contents. We offer off-site humidity controlled storage for all your belongings. Our construction crews will work diligently to restore the property to its former appeal in a timely workmanlike manner. We work with all major insurer carriers to ensure the scope of work is accurate and all Provide consultation 24/7 regarding structural integrity and impact of damages. Retain Engineer as needed to verify extent of loss. Coordinate with insurer and engineer to maintain Critical Path of project and restoration. work meets codes.

Hail Damage:

Your roof is one of the single most critical systems on your home. During a hail-storm, the life-span of your existing roof can get greatly reduced in just (1) storm. Hail deteriorates shingles, dents and dings metal, breaks vinyl, and creates many weatherization concerns. We document the conditions, develop a scope of work necessary to effectively repair the damages, and bill the insurance company directly.

Mold Abatement:

Mold can begin to grow within 24-48 hours of proper conditions. NYS now requires an Assessment of Mold which was legislation implemented in January of 2016 in NYS DOL. We are a NYS Licensed Mold Abatement Contractor. A 3rd Party Mold Assessment firm must be retained to scope-write the mold. Sampling and testing is often required.

Sewer Back-up:

In the event of a sewer back-up it is imperative the clean-up begin as quickly as possible (while maintaining safe conditions for all involved) as to reduce likeliness of mold growth and further contamination. We employ an in-house crew of Plumbers to ensure repairs can be made to prevent further loss.

Temporary Power & Heat:

ABC Emergency can be dispatched 24/7 to provide adequate sized power generation for all of your needs (industrial and commercial as well). We also can provide temporary heating for projects to ensure occupied-spaces maintain safe temperatures.

Tree Removal

ABC Emergency is a full-service emergency tree-removal specialist. We coordinate with our contractors and in-house staff to quickly handle unsafe and downed trees. We specialize in dangerous tree removals and offer crane services.
